# korin.pink

**korin.pink** is the IRC infrastructure for [Stellar](https://github.com/orphic-inc/stellar-api) — a private music community. This wiki is the community knowledge base.

---

## IRC

korin.pink runs [Ergo](https://ergo.chat/) — a modern IRCv3 server with built-in history, SASL auth, and TLS. No account required to lurk, but registering your nick lets you link it to your Stellar account and earn [IRCScore](/wiki/irc/irc-score).

→ [How to connect](/wiki/irc/connecting)  
→ [Channel directory](/wiki/irc/channels)  
→ [Community etiquette](/wiki/irc/etiquette)
